{# MacroMic wordmark — inlined, not . An -referenced SVG is an isolated document: the page's webfonts do not reach it, so 'Space Grotesk' would never load and the mark would silently fall back to the system sans. Inlined, the resolves against the fonts the page already loads. Source of truth for the artwork is app/static/brand/macromic-wordmark.svg — keep the two in sync until the text is converted to paths (see BRAND.md). The viewBox is deliberately tighter than the source file's "0 0 620 200". Measured in the browser, the artwork only occupies x 34-463, y 30-180 — the artboard carries ~31% empty margin, nearly all of it on the right. Rendered at a nav-sized height that padding shrinks the visible mark to about three quarters of its box, which read as undersized next to the nav links. Cropping to the ink plus a small margin is the fix; the geometry inside is untouched. Right and bottom edges are set by the waveform bars, whose coordinates are fixed, so they cannot shift with the font. The top edge is cap height, hence the slightly larger margin there. Sizing lives in CSS, not here.
